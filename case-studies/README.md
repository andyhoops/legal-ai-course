# Case Studies

This folder contains my structured analyses of UK contract law cases, developed with Claude as a thinking partner.

## Format

Each case study should cover:

- **Case name and citation**
- **Facts** — what happened, briefly
- **Legal issue** — what question the court had to decide
- **Decision** — what the court held and why
- **Significance** — why this case matters; what rule it established or clarified
- **My analysis** — my own take, including where I agree or disagree with the reasoning
- **How I worked with Claude** — what prompts were most useful, where Claude needed correcting

## Goal

By the end of the course: 3–5 case studies, including at least one comparative analysis of two cases.
